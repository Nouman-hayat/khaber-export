B2B e-Commerce Capability

Khyber Exports advanced e-chain systems is global in scope with standardized operating systems applicable to diverse country’s business practice. Main operation components of e-chain are integrated and composed of Air and Ocean Freight Management Systems , Logistics Warehousing Management Systems and financial & Accounting Systems. By incorporating the replication technology, Khyber Exports has achieved its goal of localized data processing and centralized data warehousing on a real time basis.

Culture Efficient Internal Information Sharing and Automatic Data Integration Functionalities

Khyber Exports e-chain systems strengthens a wide range of business functions like operational efficiency , customer service enhancement and management performance. It not only strong links to all Khyber Exports stations but also smoothes the information –sharing & communication process via the delicate intranet and central database. Customer related data as well as business development information are well integrated and can be retrieved from the automation systems to achieve any time-definite work.

External Reporting and Data Exchange Capabilities

The web-based supply chain visibility of the e-chain systems has been built based on the customer-oriented profile with up-to-date, end-to-end air and ocean freight shipment status tracking , and inventory and warehousing transaction status reporting. Realizing the value of data integration and equipped with the project experiences and knowledge , Khyber Exports has not only proactively participated, but also aggressively promoted many value-generating projects in streamlining supply chain visibility , improving productivity efficiency, as well as driving total cost saving. Numerous customization projects of data integration between Khyber Exports e-chain and major customer’s systems with mechanisms in XML,FLAT FILE and some in traditional EDI have been conducted , covering the scope from freight management systems, warehouse management systems, to automate payment approvals & processes. We have experienced several major data exchange project for high technology companies.

In addition , the integration of transmitting necessary information of e-manifest among all offices and with local Customs strategically has increased our overall service performance , shortened the lead-time, and achieved its operational efficiency.
