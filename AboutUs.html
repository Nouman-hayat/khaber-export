About Us

Established in 2000 , Khyber Exports have maintained a proactive role in the evolving international transportation and logistics market. Our objective is to be the most competitive global transportation and total logistics service provider. With this objective in mind, we are committed to the continuous search for excellence that offers efficient and effective total solutions. Khyber Exports dedicates itself and its resources to meet the diverse needs of our customers with flexibility in a cost conscious and professional manner.

Throughout years we have progressively expanded our service network on global scale. At the same time we have developed and upgraded our e-commerce platform, Khyber Exports e-chain systems to meet the global needs of our customers. Our efforts are not only to integrate our internal data and information to strengthen overall management performance, but to also assist our customers to increase their competitiveness in the market by providing external data integration and on-line real time information visibility for effective supply chain management.
