Air Freight

Khyber Exports air freight service offers scheduled consolidation services, or direct air carrier , airport to airport and door to door delivery features. Real time on line tracking systems enables customers to obtain or monitor accurately the actual status of their freight while it move through the logistics supply chain cycle times.

Ocean Freight

Khyber Exports ocean freight systems, begun as supplemental services to air freight business, has become an independent full-service division. Working closely with a handful of selective carriers to enhance our quality services, we provide effective logistics solutions at lower costs that help our customers grow and stay competitive in today’s market place.

Customs Brokerage

Khyber Exports experienced, licensed country wide customs brokers will provide our customers hassle-free customs clearance services and professional solutions customized to specific customer’s logistics requirements.

Warehousing and Distributions

Khyber Exports offers a full range of warehousing and distribution functions and services, they include distribution assortment, cross-docking, “just-in-time”, sequenced supply synchronized delivery management, and in-transit mixing product support.
